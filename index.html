--- layout: home title: Fluffy Paw Books subtitle: Gentle animal stories for children ages 4–8 --- # Fluffy Paw Books A home for slow, heartfelt children's stories about animals, imagination, and emotional growth. --- ## 📚 Our First Series ### The Adventures of Sammy Sammy is a real black German Shepherd with one floppy ear who lives in the Tennessee hills. He is the first character in the Fluffy Paw Books universe. --- ## 🌿 Story Philosophy Each story begins with a simple animal adventure: creek running, exploring, playing, and resting in nature. Then it gently opens into emotional themes children experience but may not yet have words for. --- ## 🐾 The Floppy Ear Idea Different is not something to fix. It is something to understand. Sammy's floppy ear represents the idea that differences can be strengths when met with kindness and honesty. --- ## 📖 What's Next More animal characters and story series will be added as Fluffy Paw Books grows.
